📌 Roadmap — Project Evolution

Step by step, Aurum Token evolves into a complete financial ecosystem.

Phase 1 — Presale ✅ (Q1 2025)

  • Launch of the official Aurum Token (AUR) presale contract.
  • 200,000 AUR available in batches.
  • Verified smart contract on BNB Smart Chain.
  • First community members joining.

Phase 2 — PancakeSwap Launch 🔒 (Q2 2025)

  • Listing of the AUR/BNB pair on PancakeSwap.
  • 300,000 AUR locked as liquidity.
  • Price stabilization around $0.10.
  • Transparent liquidity lock for investor trust.

Phase 3 — MiniSwap ⚡ (Q4 2025)

  • Development of a mini-swap inside the Aurum official website.
  • Direct AUR ⇆ BNB swaps.
  • Simple, fast, and user-friendly interface.
  • Immediate access without external platforms.

Phase 4 — Staking ⛓️ (Q2 2026)

  • Launch of staking pools for long-term holders.
  • Lock AUR tokens for set periods.
  • Receive passive rewards.
  • Strengthen community loyalty and token stability.

Phase 5 — AurumSwap (DEX) 🌐 (Q1 2027)

  • Full development of AurumSwap, a decentralized exchange.
  • AUR at the center of a new ecosystem.
  • Liquidity pools and farming.
  • Advanced tools for trading and growth.
  • Expansion beyond BNB Smart Chain.

Phase 6 — Treasury & Payments 💳 (Q4 2027)

  • Establishing treasury management for long-term sustainability.
  • Growth of a financial reserve for stability.
  • Integration of bank card payments (Visa/Mastercard) to buy AUR directly.
  • Strategic partnerships for adoption in real-world payments.
  • Future listings on centralized exchanges (CEX).

Phase 7 — Global Expansion 🌍 (Q2 2028)

  • Partnerships with international organizations.
  • Entry into new markets worldwide.
  • Stronger community presence in key regions.

Phase 8 — Social Programs 🎓🍞 (Q4 2028)

  • Launch of education and essentials programs in underprivileged regions.
  • Long-term impact: reinvesting part of Aurum’s success back into communities.
  • Support for countries historically deprived of their natural wealth.
