📄 Aurum Token — Whitepaper

A complete, high-level view of Aurum’s vision, structure, and long-term plan.

1. Introduction

Aurum Token (AUR) is built on the BNB Smart Chain with a fixed total supply of 1,000,000 AUR. Inspired by the timeless strength and scarcity of gold, Aurum brings solid tokenomics together with real usability across DeFi and future integrations.

Smart-contract link (BscScan): 0xad8e960ae1d06b5ab4b49a872649e90af040f91d .

2. Vision & Mission

  • Beyond a token: build a robust ecosystem: presale → PancakeSwap → MiniSwap → Staking → DEX.
  • Global inclusion: bring “digital gold” value to anyone, anywhere.
  • Durability & growth: mechanisms designed for long-term holders and sustainable expansion.
  • Social impact (long-term): education & essentials initiatives in regions historically deprived of their natural wealth.

3. Token Distribution

  • Presale: 200,000 AUR
  • Liquidity: 300,000 AUR
  • Team (vesting): 150,000 AUR
  • Marketing: 100,000 AUR
  • Staking: 100,000 AUR
  • Treasury: 100,000 AUR
  • Burn (manual & gradual): 50,000 AUR

A full interactive allocation chart is available on the Tokenomics page.

4. Roadmap — High-Level

Phase 1 — Presale ✅
Official smart contract presale on our site.
Phase 2 — PancakeSwap 🔒
AUR/BNB listing with 300k AUR locked as liquidity.
Phase 3 — MiniSwap ⚡
Simple AUR ⇆ BNB swap integrated into our site.
Phase 4 — Staking ⛓️
Reward pools for holders who lock AUR.
Phase 5 — AurumSwap (DEX) 🌐
Full DEX with liquidity pools, farming, and pro tools.
Phase 6 — Treasury & Payments 💳
Treasury operations (100k AUR) + card rails (Visa/Mastercard) integrations.
Phase 7 — Expansion 🌍
CEX listings, strategic partnerships, and global marketing.
Phase 8 — Social Programs 🎓🍞
Education & essentials programs funded from dedicated allocations.

For a more detailed, phase-by-phase view, see the Roadmap page.

5. Security & Transparency

  • Verified smart contract on BNB Smart Chain.
  • Liquidity locked after PancakeSwap listing.
  • Manual, transparent, gradual burn of 50,000 AUR.
  • Clear communications through official channels.
  • Planned third-party audit (e.g., CertiK) as milestones progress.

6. Conclusion

Aurum is more than a project; it’s a journey of trust, growth, and real-world utility. By uniting the timeless idea of gold with Web3, AUR aims to be a long-lasting store of value and a practical medium of exchange. We invite the community to build this legacy together.
